Frequently Asked Questions

What is Final Strike?
Final Strike is a multiplayer gaming community that has revived the action-packed IGI 2 multiplayer, providing a platform for both veterans and newcomers.
How do I set up the game?
You can follow our step-by-step guide on the How To Setup page to install and configure the game.
Where can I download the game?
The game files and updates are available for download on our Downloads page.
Are there any new maps available?
Yes! Check out the latest maps and community creations on our Maps page.
Is Final Strike free?
Yes, Final Strike is completely free to download and play.
